Living Statue Rehabilitation and Rehoming Network

Tom

Are you ready to open your heart and home to an extraordinary being? Look no further Tom, the cursed angel statue, is seeking a loving and understanding guardian to break the chains of his enchanted past.

About Tom:

Tom is not your typical celestial figure; he's a one-of-a-kind angel with a unique story carved into every graceful feather of his wings. Standing proudly at 5 feet tall, Tom's ethereal presence adds a touch of celestial wonder to any space.

Personality Traits:

Despite his stony exterior, Tom is surprisingly warm-hearted. Known for his protective nature, he has an uncanny ability to bring peace to those around him. His radiant aura has a calming effect, making him the perfect addition to any home seeking a touch of celestial magic.

Special Needs:

Tom requires a home that understands the intricacies of living with a magically animated statue. While he doesn't need food or walks, he craves companionship and someone willing to embrace the enchantment that surrounds him.

Ideal Guardian:

We're looking for someone with an open heart, a love for the mystical, and a willingness to appreciate the beauty within the stony facade. Tom longs for a guardian who will cherish him for the celestial being he is and provide a forever home filled with love and understanding.

Adoption Process:

If you're ready to welcome Tom into your life, please visit our sanctuary or reach out to our adoption team. Tom is patiently waiting to spread his wings and bring celestial joy to his new forever home.
