A Little About Me

I am very interested in the topic of information. This is interest led me to an interest in linguistics and especially in Semantics, that is the study of meaning, as well as General Semantics. General Semantics which was developed by Alfred Korzybski, and English(or E-)Prime, which was developed by D. David Bourland Jr., a student of Korzybski's.

E-Prime

E-Prime is a version of English that excludes all forms of the verb "to be," such as "is," "are," "am," "was," "were," "be," and their various conjugations and contractions. The goal of E-Prime is to encourage clearer communication by eliminating subjective assertions, generalizations, and judgments.
